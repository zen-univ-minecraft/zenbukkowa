# Winner Determination

## Goal

Determine and announce the winner.

## Ranking

1. Sort players by total cumulative points (sum of all 5 categories) descending.
2. Tie-breaker: higher `MINERAL` points.
3. Second tie-breaker: higher `VOID` points.

## Announcement

- Server-wide title: `Winner: {Name}`
- Subtitle: `{Total} Points`
- Chat broadcast of top 3 with ranks and totals.

## Post-Event

- Scoreboard remains visible showing final `Time: Finished`.
- Breaks still grant points but do not affect winner.
- Menu still functions for stat viewing.
