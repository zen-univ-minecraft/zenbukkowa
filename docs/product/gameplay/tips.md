# Tips

## Early Game (0-15 min)

1. Buy `AREA_RADIUS` tier 1 immediately for 500 TERRA points.
2. Buy `HASTE_AURA` tier 1 for 500 MINERAL points.
3. Mine stone and surface ores to build both TERRA and MINERAL balances.

## Mid Game (15-60 min)

4. Push `AREA_RADIUS` and `AREA_DEPTH` to tier 3 for 5x5x5 breaking.
5. Buy `TIDE_BREAKER` and clear an Ocean Monument for the +5000 bonus.
6. Balance all five categories so you can afford skills in every tree.

## Late Game (60-120 min)

7. Max out area skills to 9x9x9 for massive throughput.
8. Buy `VOID_SIPHON` and mine in the Nether/End for boosted VOID points.
9. Destroy a Woodland Mansion for another +5000 bonus.
10. Check the scoreboard to see which categories need more focus.

## General

- Do not neglect ORGANIC and AQUATIC. Many players focus only on TERRA/MINERAL and get stuck on prerequisites.
- Use the Help menu in-game for quick reference.
- Toggle language in Settings if you prefer Japanese.
