# Gameplay Tips

## Goal

Give players actionable advice for maximizing points.

## Tips

1. Buy `AREA_RADIUS` first; it unlocks every other branch.
2. Upgrade `AREA_DEPTH` after radius to increase vertical destruction.
3. Invest in every domain's area skill: BLAST_MINING, WILD_GROWTH, TSUNAMI, VOID_RIFT, HARVEST_WAVE.
4. Ancient debris gives 20 MINERAL points per block.
5. Ocean monuments are the best AQUATIC source.
6. Balance all six categories so you can afford skills in every tree.
7. Use `FORTUNE_TOUCH` before targeting ore-rich areas.
8. Structure bonuses are one-time; race other players to claim them.
9. Save up for mythic skills — ANGEL_WINGS and TITAN_STRIKE are game-changers.
10. Operators can adjust the global point multiplier from the settings menu.
