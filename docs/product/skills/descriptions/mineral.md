# MINERAL Skills

### HASTE_AURA
- **Mechanic:** Permanent Haste effect at all times.
- **Tier I:** Haste I.
- **Tier V:** Haste V.
- **Tip:** Buy this early; EFFICIENCY requires it.

### FORTUNE_TOUCH
- **Mechanic:** Chance to double points on any block break.
- **Tier I:** 10% chance.
- **Tier II:** 20% chance.
- **Tier III:** 30% chance.
- **Prerequisite:** HASTE_AURA tier 3 for tier 3.

### VEIN_MINER
- **Mechanic:** Extra points per ore block broken.
- **Tier 1:** +1 point.
- **Tier 2:** +2 points.
- **Tier 3:** +3 points.
- **Prerequisite:** FORTUNE_TOUCH tier 2.

### MAGNET
- **Mechanic:** Auto-collects dropped items within 5 blocks.
- **Prerequisite:** VEIN_MINER tier 1.

### CRYSTAL_VISION
- **Mechanic:** Highlights ores within a radius.
- **Tier 1:** 5 blocks.
- **Tier 2:** 10 blocks.
- **Tier 3:** 15 blocks.
- **Prerequisite:** MAGNET.

### BLAST_MINING
- **Mechanic:** When breaking ore blocks, expands the break radius by bonus tiers.
- **Tier 1:** +1 bonus radius.
- **Tier 2:** +2 bonus radius.
- **Tier 3:** +3 bonus radius.
- **Prerequisite:** CRYSTAL_VISION.
